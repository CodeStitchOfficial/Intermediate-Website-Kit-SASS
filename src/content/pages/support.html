Your domain helps people find you, pick a good one

Computers use numbers, but people need words. Every website has a numerical IP address. For example, no one would ever remember "208.80.153.224/27"---we use wikipedia.org. This word-based version is the domain. The Domain Name System (DNS) translates the readable domain into the IP address. Your domain is how people will remember your website, so pick a good one!

Every website needs an active domain (it expires if you forget to renew it). You also need to maintain your DNS records to configure your website and email. Some common DNS records include:

  • A record: to assign your website's IP address to your domain;
  • CNAME record: to add an alias or alternate version of your domain;
  • MX record: to direct mail to your email server; and
  • TXT record: to prove you own the domain and that your email's aren't spam.

There are many more types of DNS records. If this stuff gives you a headache, we can manage your domain for you for $20 a year. We'll return your domain to you without hassle should you cancel our service. You always own your domain, even if we buy it for you.

Hosting connects your website with the world

Web hosting connects your website to the internet. Your website's files live on the host's server. When someone tries to view your website, their computer pings the host's server and it returns the files to them.

A content delivery network or CDN works with the web host to improve website performance. A CDN is a global network of servers that can serve a cached version of your website. This can make the website faster and more secure as well as reduce the likelihood of it crashing.

Our web host offers a CDN at no extra charge. We include hosting with our Subscription websites---you're already covered if you subscribe. If you buy a Standard website, you can add hosting for $25 a month.

Clear copy converts

Don't make your customers think. Reading your website should be effortless. It also needs to be persuasive. Storytelling accomplishes this.

Your website, and your brand, needs to tell the story of a hero. In this story, the hero overcomes obstacles, solves their problems, and transforms themself. Your business is not the hero, your customer is. You're the mentor who guides them to success.

This narrative structure puts your customer first. Customers don't care about you, they care about how you can solve their problems. This approach shows how your product or service will improve their life. Features and benefits are important, but on their own they aren't convincing. You need to show them how your offer will transform their life for the better.

Clear stories engage your customer and persuade them to act. Don't settle for bland copy that lists boring facts and fails to inspire action. You have a great offer, present it in a way that lets it shine.

We've applied this approach throughout our own website. If you have us write your copy, we can do the same for you. We'll apply the same framework along with keyword research and strong calls to action.

Also, our Brand Narrative service gives you the narrative framework to use on your own. Use clear, consistent, and coordinated messaging across every channel to represent your brand.
