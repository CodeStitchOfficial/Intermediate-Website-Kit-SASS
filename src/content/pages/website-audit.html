What the DIY Disaster Detector will uncover

The DIY Disaster Detector uses a rubric to test website fundamentals. This rubric enables me to quickly and objectively determine the quality of a website.

  • Technical Problems

    The speed, mobile, and accessibility issues that make your website a pain to use. 40% of people leave if your site takes more than 3 seconds to load.

  • Conversion Problems

    The poor calls-to-action and weak messaging that make your website confusing. Can visitors quickly find what you do, who you serve, and how to buy?

  • Credibility Problems

    The design choices and trust-signals that undermine your professional credibility. Does your website make you look as competent as you actually are?
