About the Founder

Serving people, not profits

Hi, my name is Tianna Hutchins, and I am an unapologetic cat lover, history nerd, and public servant. In 2018, I joined the U.S. Department of State's web team as a web editor. Today, I'm the team lead overseeing a small team that manages the Department's main website. The website receives millions of visits each month and peaked at over 47,500 pages. Over the past seven years, I've learned a lot. Now, I am a jack-of-all-trades in the world of websites.

For the next chapter in my career, I want to share my knowledge and experience with people like you. I could look for a corporate job, but I would like to use my skills instead to help small businesses grow. That's why I started Neva Masquerade.

You don't have to settle for a subpar website and weak branding. I'm ready to help you elevate your brand and grow your business. Are you?

About Neva Masquerade

Be bold. Be unique. Be you.

How can you shine when your online presence feels like a masquerade? Starting a business is challenging. Feeling lost, overwhelmed, and sometimes like an imposter is easy. Running a business takes a lot of time. You don't have time to grapple with a subpar website and an inconsistent brand. Neva Masquerade was born out of a desire to ease this burden.

Many small businesses struggle to make their mark, but it doesn't have to be that way. Our custom-coded websites can free you from the hassle of web design so that you can focus on what you do best. We aspire to help businesses like yours thrive.

We don't use templates or page builders because every web design should be a partnership. Your website should reflect your vision and personality and not a generic knock-off. Your business deserves to stand out and not blend into the crowded marketplace. We're excited to see what we can build together. When can we start?
