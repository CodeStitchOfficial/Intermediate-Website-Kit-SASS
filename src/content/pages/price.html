Web design

We build and run simple, 5-page static websites for service-based small businesses. There are two pricing models for this service. The first, is a Standard Website that you buy outright and pay for upfront. The second is a Subscription Website with a low monthly payment. You may also add-on extra services or features to either website package.

Standard Website Starts at $3,000

Ideal for business owners who can afford to pay for the website upfront and prefer to own the copyright. You can host and maintain it yourself, but we don't recommend that. We encourage you to add Hosting and Unlimited Content Edits.

Includes:

  • Homepage and 4 content pages (About, Services, Contact, etc.)
  • Personalized design
  • Hand-coded website developed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Responsive, mobile-first design
    • PageSpeed Insights scores in the 90s or 100
    • Meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards (accessible to people with disabilities)
  • Copyright rights to the design and codebase

Does not include:

  • Hosting (add $25/month)
  • Unlimited content edits (add $50/month)
  • Extra pages (add $100 fee for each page)
  • Blog and training session (add $250 fee)
  • Dark mode (add $150 fee)
  • Design refreshes

Subscription Website $175 / month

Ideal for business owners who can't afford a large, upfront cost. It's also ideal if you would like to redesign your website every three years or so for no extra cost. Think of it as going from one car lease to the next, but without a new contract. This gives you ongoing value without charging you more. The initial contract is for 12 months, after that we go month to month and you may cancel at any time.

To make this model work, we keep the copyright on subscription websites. Your domain and content you provided is always yours; the design and code are ours. This prevents people from canceling and keeping the website for a fraction of the cost. We want to build strong partnerships that last for years, not churn through lots of clients.

Includes:

  • Homepage and 4 content pages (About, Services, Contact, etc.)
  • Personalized design
  • Hand-coded website developed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Responsive, mobile-first design
    • PageSpeed Insights scores in the 90s or 100
    • Meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards (accessible to people with disabilities)
  • Hosting ($25 value)
  • Unlimited content edits ($50 value)
  • Design refreshes every ~3 years

Does not include:

  • Copyright rights to the design and codebase
  • Extra pages (add $100 fee for each page)
  • Blog and training session (add $250 fee)
  • Dark mode (add $150 fee)

Figma Design Starts at $500

This option is for business owners who have a developer, but no designer. Figma is the industry standard platform for web designs and prototypes. We'll create an annotated design in Figma for your developer to use. Our design will include 4 breakpoints to ensure it looks good on different devices. The total price will depend on:

  • the number of page designs you need,
  • the complexity of the content and sections, and
  • if you'd like extra rounds of revisions.

Add-ons and other services

Both website packages include 5 custom designed and custom coded pages. You may choose to add more features and services to either package.

Extra Pages $100 / each

Because we don't use page builders, we have to create the HTML for every page on the website (except for blog posts). This fee covers the extra time and effort that goes in to creating each extra page. Once we've built the page, it's covered under Unlimited Content Edits and there won't be any more charges for it.

Blog $250

Blogging can be a great way to improve your website's search engine optimization (SEO). We can add a blog for you to manage yourself. To do this, we use Decap CMS. It works with static websites (like what we build). Unlike WordPress, it doesn't have a database so it's lower maintenance and more secure. It's also super easy to use.

This fee covers configuring Decap CMS, styling the blog, and a training session for you to learn how to use it.

Dark Mode $150

Up your website's wow factor by adding a dark mode toggle to it. Dark mode adds a modern feature that your competitors may not have on their own websites. It may also offer benefits to people with certain eye conditions. This fee covers the extra CSS work we need to do to add dark mode styles to your entire website.

Support

(Included with subscription website)

Hosting is what puts your website on the internet and makes it available to your customers. You can choose to host your Standard Website yourself. If you do, you'll need to set up the right tech stack and if something goes wrong you'll have to troubleshoot it yourself. If you let us handle your hosting, any problems will be on us to solve.

Unlimited Content Edits $50 / month

(Included with subscription website)

Websites aren't carved in stone, we can update them as often as needed. This small monthly retainer covers all updates you may need to your existing pages. For example, replacing images, changing your business hours, or updating text. We don't bill you for the time these updates take, it's all completely covered by the retainer.

Branding

The word "branding" might make you think of companies like Coca-Cola, Nike, or Apple. But it isn't something that only large corporations can have—small businesses are brands too. You also don't need a large budget to build your brand. Money helps, of course, but as long as you are consistent and strategic you'll do better than everyone who isn't.

We can help you be more consistent with all your communications. While we are a web design agency first, websites aren't isolated. A strong website looks and sounds like your social media and printed materials. A weak website clashes with everything else you're doing. To that end, we also offer limited graphic design services to help you build a stronger brand.

Graphic Design Services

Use it to drive potential leads into your marketing funnel. Tell potential lead about your unique selling proposition and give them a call-to-action. We can combine this copy with elements from your website to create a compelling banner.
