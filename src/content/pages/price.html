Web design

Small businesses don't need complicated websites or a content management system. WordPress is high-maintenance and overkill for our clients' needs. Do you enjoy spending more time and money on your website than you need to?

Standard website Starts at $3,000

Do you have a good amount of cash in your budget and want to own your website? If so, the standard website is right for you.

You pay for the standard website upfront and own everything.

You can host and maintain it yourself, but we don't recommend that. It's best to add hosting and unlimited content edits.

Includes:

  • Homepage and 4 content pages (e.g., About, Services, Contact, etc.)
  • Personalized design
  • Hand-coded website developed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Responsive, mobile-first design (looks great on phones)
    • PageSpeed Insights scores of 90+ (loads fast and performs well)
    • Meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards (accessible to people with disabilities)
  • Ownership of the design and codebase

Does not include:

  • Hosting (add $25/month)
  • Unlimited content edits (add $50/month)
  • Extra pages (add $100 fee for each page)
  • Blog and training session (add $250 fee)
  • Dark mode (add $150 fee)
  • Design refreshes

Subscription website $175 / month

Do you have a limited budget and no upfront capital? Do you want to refresh your website's style every few years without paying extra? If you answer yes, the subscription website fits your needs.

You'll pay for your website monthly, and over time, you'll pay for it in full. To give you continued value, we'll let you redesign your website every few years at no extra charge. This frugal option will keep your website fresh and modern.

The subscription also includes hosting and unlimited content edits. This adds even more value to your monthly payment.

To make this model work, we own the website. Your domain and the content you provide are always yours, but the design and code are ours. We do this to protect our investment in you. Building a website without upfront payment is a gamble. If you cancel after the first year, we won't receive payment for all our time and labor.

Includes:

  • Homepage and 4 content pages (About, Services, Contact, etc.)
  • Personalized design
  • Hand-coded website developed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Responsive, mobile-first design (looks great on phones)
    • PageSpeed Insights scores of 90+ (loads fast and performs well)
    • Meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards (accessible to people with disabilities)
  • Hosting ($25 value)
  • Unlimited content edits ($50 value)
  • Design refreshes every ~3 years ($3,000 value)

Does not include:

  • Ownership of the design and codebase
  • Extra pages (add $100 fee for each page)
  • Blog and training session (add $250 fee)
  • Dark mode (add $150 fee)

Figma design Starts at $500

Do you have a developer and need a web designer? If so, a Figma design is the solution you need.

Figma is the industry standard platform for web designs and prototypes. We'll create an annotated design in Figma for your developer to use. Our design will include 4 breakpoints to ensure it looks good on different devices. The total price will depend on:

  • the number of page designs you need,
  • the complexity of the content and sections and
  • if you need more than 2 rounds of revisions.

Add-ons

Both website packages include 5 custom-designed and custom-coded pages. You can add more features and services to either package. All are one-time charges.

Extra pages $100 / each

Do you need more than 5 pages?

We can create as many as you need. After we've built the page, we'll never charge you for working on it again.

This fee covers the time and effort required to create each extra page.

Blog $250

Do you want to blog to improve your website's search engine optimization (SEO)?

We can add a blog for you to manage yourself. To do this, we use Decap CMS. It works with static websites (like what we build). Unlike WordPress, it doesn't have a database, so it has lower maintenance and is more secure. It's also super easy to use.

This fee covers configuring Decap CMS, styling the blog, and a training session on how to use it.

Dark mode $150

Are you a fan of dark mode? Do you want to wow people?

Dark mode adds a modern feature your competitors may not have on their websites. It can also benefit people with certain eye conditions. It's a good feature.

This fee covers the extra CSS work required to add dark mode styles to your website.

Support

Your website needs don't end with design and development. Websites demand continuous maintenance and support, as well as good content. Do you have someone experienced in this line of work?

Domain management $20 / year

Do you know what a domain is? What about DNS records?

Your domain is like your website's name. You need to renew it yearly, or it will expire and break your website. The Domain Name System (DNS) connects your website and email to your domain.

We can manage all this for you for a small annual fee.

Hosting $25 / month

(Included with subscription website)

Do you know what a web host is or how it works?

Don't worry, most non-technical people don't. Hosting is what puts your website on the Internet and makes it available to your customers.

You can choose to host your Standard Website yourself. If you do, you must set up the right tech stack and troubleshoot it if something goes wrong.

Or, we can do it all for you. If a problem comes up, we'll take care of it. You don't need to do a thing.

Unlimited content edits $50 / month

(Included with subscription website)

Do you want to make Google happy by keeping your website up-to-date?

Regular updates can improve your ranking in search results. This monthly retainer covers all updates to your existing pages.

We'll take care of everything and won't charge for the time it takes. The retainer covers it all.

Copywriting $0.50 / word

Do you know how to write persuasive copy?

A website is only as good as its content. The most stunning website with perfect code won't be effective if the content sucks. Brand storytelling offers a proven framework that engages and persuades.

We'll use this approach to turn boring features and benefits into a compelling story. If you add our brand narrative service, we'll give you these talking points and tips on how to use them on your own.

Branding

Style guides are a guardrail. They keep you consistent. They make design workflows more efficient and streamline decision-making. They teach newcomers how to create content for your brand. You can't overvalue a style guide.

  • Company mission, vision, and values
  • Brand narrative (if applicable)
  • Tone and voice
  • Typography
  • Logo usage
  • Color palette
  • Imagery
  • Website
  • Templates (if applicable)

Brand narrative $500

Does your current brand tell a story? Does it engage people and inspire them to take action?

Stories sell, and emotions drive decisions, not reason. We use facts to justify our choices after we've already made them. A brand narrative lets you capitalize on this human nature with your messaging.

Tell this story everywhere. Any time your business comes up in conversation, pull from your brand narrative.

We apply this strategy to our copywriting; we'll give you the talking points to use.
